F.A.Q

How does this work?

Our smile training program uses proven techniques developed by professional smile coaches. You'll learn through interactive video lessons, practice exercises, and personalized feedback.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our paid courses. If you're not completely satisfied with your progress, simply contact our support team for a full refund.

How long are the courses?

Course lengths vary depending on the topic and difficulty level. Most individual tutorials range from 5-15 minutes, while complete courses can take 2-4 hours to finish.

Do I need special equipment?

No special equipment is required! All you need is a device with internet access and preferably a mirror to practice with. Some advanced courses may recommend using a camera.
